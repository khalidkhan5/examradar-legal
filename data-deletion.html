Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: April 3, 2026

At ExamRadar, we respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to request deletion of your data from our systems.

Option 1: Delete Account from the App

The easiest way to delete your data is directly from the app:

1 Open ExamRadar and go to the Profile tab

2 Scroll down to Account section

3 Tap Delete Account

4 Confirm the deletion when prompted

This will permanently delete your account and all associated data from our servers.

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request data deletion by sending an email to:

Email: khalidkhan753@gmail.com

Subject: ExamRadar Data Deletion Request

Please include the email address associated with your ExamRadar account so we can identify and delete your data.

We will process email deletion requests within 30 days and send you a confirmation once completed.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

What Is NOT Deleted

  • Anonymized analytics data — Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics may be retained for service improvement.
  • Data held by third parties — Google (AdMob, FCM, Sign-In) may retain data independently per their own privacy policies. You can manage your Google data at Google Data & Privacy.
  • Local device data — Shared Preferences and cached data on your device can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

Processing Time

  • In-app deletion: Immediate
  • Email request: Within 30 days
